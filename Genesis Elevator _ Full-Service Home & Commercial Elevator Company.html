THE GENESIS WAY

 We focus on earning our customer’s trust and providing the best possible customer experience.

Genesis Service Principles

Fast Service

Our 24/7 call center is always ready to dispatch a certified technician to stay within our 24-Hour Fix Rule. We use GPS and scheduling technology to get your problem fixed within 24 hours.

Reliability

Genesis takes a first-fix-focus. This means we make sure to fix your problem on the first visit. If we don’t, we return to make it right. Choose an elevator company that you can rely on.

Enhanced Communication

Expect live updates via text and email once your elevator technician has been scheduled, is en route and has completed the repair. Our customer service team is available 24/7 for any questions or concerns.

Safe Equipment

Our team of NAEC certified elevator technicians are qualified to fix and prevent malfunction. We provide the industry’s most trusted brands like Vertical Express, Home Elevators Direct, Pneumatic Vacuum Elevator and Garaventa.
